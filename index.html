The money, worked out

Built for the Australian financial year.

  • Gross, net and tax to the day. Earnings tracked as each contract runs, not reconstructed at year end.
  • GST, super and ATO deductions at current rates. Every record freezes the rate that applied the day you logged it, so updating a rate never rewrites your history.
  • An earned-to-date figure that respects shutdowns. The curve goes flat across a holiday break instead of overstating what you have earned.
  • A what-if calculator and clean CSV exports for weighing up a new offer and handing figures to your accountant.
