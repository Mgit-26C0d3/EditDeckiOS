Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device. EditDeck is local-first and offline, with no accounts and no servers, so nothing you enter is uploaded or synced anywhere.

Do I need an account or an internet connection?

No. There's nothing to sign in to, and the app works fully offline.

How do I back up my data or move to a new device?

Use the backup feature to export a single .editdeck file containing everything. Save it wherever you like, then use restore on another device to bring it all back. Backups happen only when you choose to make one.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

Because your data lives only on the device, removing the app removes its stored data. Export a backup first if you want to keep a copy.

Which tax rates does EditDeck use?

EditDeck is tuned to the Australian freelance context and the July–June financial year. It applies current ATO figures for mileage and work-from-home deductions, GST, and super. Historical records keep the rate that applied when you created them, so updating rates never rewrites past entries.

Why does the app ask for microphone, photos, or notification access?

Only to power specific features, and only on your device: the microphone records voice memos for your notes, photo access adds images you pick to your notes and library, and notifications show local reminders for your events. None of it is ever sent anywhere.

How do I request a feature or report a problem?

Email madebymikesupport@icloud.com. Feature requests genuinely shape what gets built next.

Is my data private?

Yes. EditDeck collects nothing. You can read the full privacy policy for the details.
