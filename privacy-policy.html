EditDeck

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

EditDeck keeps everything on your device. It has no accounts, no servers, and no analytics. It does not collect, transmit, or share your data.

The short version

EditDeck is a local-first, offline app. All of your information lives on your device and never leaves it unless you choose to export and share a backup yourself. There is nothing to sign in to, no data sent to us or anyone else, and no tracking of any kind.

What we collect

Nothing. EditDeck collects no personal information. We operate no servers and have no way to receive, see, or store anything you enter into the app.

What stays on your device

Everything you create in EditDeck is stored only in the app's local storage on your device, including:

  • Productions, schedules, and calendar events
  • Financial figures such as rates, tax, expenses, mileage, and work-from-home records
  • Contacts you add
  • Notes, audio recordings, images, and imported files and receipts

This information is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted off your device by EditDeck.

Device permissions

EditDeck asks for a few system permissions so certain features can work. In every case the data is used only on your device and is never sent anywhere:

  • Microphone — to record voice memos you attach to your notes. Recordings are saved on your device.
  • Photos — to add images you choose to your notes and library, and to save images you create. EditDeck only accesses the items you select.
  • Notifications — to show local reminders for your events and deadlines. These are scheduled on your device.

Backups

EditDeck lets you create a backup file (.editdeck) so you can keep a copy of your data or move it to a new device. Backups are created only when you ask, and they are handled entirely by you — where you save them and whether you share them is your choice. EditDeck does not upload backups anywhere.

Third-party code

EditDeck uses a small open-source library (ZIPFoundation) to read and write backup files on your device. It is a file-handling component only — it collects no data and sends nothing anywhere. EditDeck contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Controlling and deleting your data

Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in control of it. You can edit or delete records within the app at any time, and deleting EditDeck from your device removes its stored data.

Children's privacy

EditDeck collects no personal information from anyone, including children. No data is gathered, transmitted, or shared regardless of who uses the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date above. Because EditDeck collects no data, any changes are expected to be minor.

Contact
